Archers is a class that can engage in ranged combat.

Archers who are master marksmen use a plethora of different shots to take down their prey, from venomous barbs and magical projectiles to explosive volleys and penetrating powershots. Archers are also masters of survival, and their familiarity with traps

Archers have a powerful connection to the wild, and an uncanny ability to befriend even the wildest of beasts.

Strong against: Mage

Weak against: Warrior
